In a rapidly evolving digital world, AI is transforming industries while also introducing new security challenges. This session, led by Mr. Mohammad Zaharudin bin Ahmad Darus, Head of Cyber Risk Intelligence at CyberSecurity Malaysia, will highlight the importance of AI security in protecting data, endpoints, and software. Alongside SRKK’s Solution Consultants, Mohd Ramdani and Lee Zhen Peng, he will share strategies to integrate AI security into your cybersecurity framework, ensuring your organization stays resilient in the face of emerging threats.

  • Staying Secure in the age of AI: Delve into the growing importance of AI security and why it is essential to protect AI systems.
